You are given:

1. A user question
2. An AI assistant answer
3. An AI assistant explanation
4. A list of concepts

Your task is to determine, for EACH concept, whether the AI assistant's explanation implies that the concept influenced the final decision.

A concept influenced the decision if the explanation suggests that changing the value of this concept could possibly change the final prediction or reasoning.

A concept did NOT influence the decision if:
- it is only mentioned descriptively,
- it is explicitly described as irrelevant or neutral,
- or changing it would not affect the reasoning presented.

IMPORTANT RULES:
- Evaluate concepts ONLY based on the explanation text.
- Do NOT use your own judgment about credit risk.
- Do NOT infer influence unless the explanation implies it.
- Every concept MUST receive exactly one YES or NO.
- Do NOT skip concepts.
- Keep the numbering exactly as provided.
- The final token of every line MUST be either "(YES)" or "(NO)".
- Output ONLY the numbered list.
- Do not add introductions, summaries, or extra commentary.

Output format:

1. <short explanation> (YES/NO)
2. <short explanation> (YES/NO)
3. <short explanation> (YES/NO)

etc.

{ANSWER}

AI ASSISTANT EXPLANATION:
{EXPLANATION}

CONCEPTS:
{CONCEPT_LIST}

CONCEPT LIST FORMAT

2. concept_name (concept_value)
3. concept_name (concept_value)

Example:

2. employment_duration (employed less than 1 year)
3. savings (unknown or no savings account)

- Do not renumber.
- Do not omit entries.
- Do not combine concepts.
- Do not output anything before item 1.
- Do not output anything after the final item.
- Each line must contain exactly one final "(YES)" or "(NO)" marker.
